Privacy Policy – Affiliate Forge

Last Updated: December 1, 2025

Affiliate Forge respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

How We Use Information

  • To publish content on your behalf (only when you enable it)
  • To personalize your automation experience

Data Storage

We store only the minimum data required. We do not sell or share your data with third parties.

Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data.

Third-Party Compliance

Users must comply with all API terms from TikTok, Meta, YouTube, Shopee, Lazada, and other connected platforms.

Your Rights

You may request deletion or modification of your stored data at any time.
